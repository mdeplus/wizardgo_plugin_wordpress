== Description ==
WizardGo Audio Player is a plugin that allows your visitors to listen to a beautiful narration of your posts.
Engage and amaze your users, allowing them to interact with your content in a new way, by listening to it.  You think this *sounds* too good to be true?  We challenge you to give it a try and draw your own conclusions!  Visitors will love to hear your content aloud!

== Installation ==
1. Upload the entire wizardgo-plaxer folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
